Frequently asked

CREATIVE AGENCY THE HAGUE, ANSWERED

What does a creative agency do?

A creative agency helps you position your brand and grow it. At Sircle Agency that means brand strategy, film, identity, websites and online growth, all under one roof.

What does a creative agency in The Hague cost?

That depends on the project. A single film or website is different from a complete brand programme. In a short call we define together what you need, and you get a proposal that fits.

What is the difference between a creative agency, advertising agency and marketing agency?

An advertising agency focuses mainly on campaigns and ads, a marketing agency on channels and results, and a creative agency on the whole brand: from strategy and film to website and growth. Sircle combines all three in one team, with film as its specialism.

Do you also make film and video?

Yes, film is our specialism. We come from the film world and produce brand films, commercials and social video with our own team in The Hague.

Do you only work in The Hague?

Our office is in Scheveningen (The Hague), and we work for companies across the Haaglanden region and throughout the Netherlands.
