Our approach

FROM INSIGHT TO ACTION

No thick reports nobody reads. Four steps, four concrete deliverables. A strategy your team can apply right away.

01 Discovery Sprint Brand Discovery Document

Kick-off workshop and a deep dive into mission, vision, audience and competitors. We get to know your brand from the inside.

02 Positioning & Strategy Brand Positioning Document

What is your brand, where do you stand, where do you need to go and what makes you different. Core values, brand promise and audience personas locked in.

03 Message & Tone of Voice Messaging Playbook

Core message, content pillars and writing style with do's & don'ts. Anyone writing for your brand writes in the right tone from the start.

04 Activation & Roadmap Brand Roadmap (90 days)

A concrete step-by-step plan: what, when, with what result. Seamless handover to SircleStudio and SircleGrowth.
