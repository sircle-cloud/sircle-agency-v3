Challenges in healthcare

THE COMPLEXITY BEHIND GOOD CARE

Healthcare organisations have unique online obstacles, compliance, trust, findability and fragmented communication. We know them.

GDPR-compliant and patient-friendly

Compliance is complex, but the site has to remain accessible and human. Privacy by design from the first line of code.

Fragmented audiences

Patients, referring physicians, family carers and healthcare professionals, all with different information needs, on one platform.

Outdated patient journey

Online appointments, intake forms and patient communication that haven't grown with how modern your care actually is.

Competition from portals

Aggregator platforms and large chains dominate search results. Local clinics need a different strategy.

Employer Branding for healthcare

Finding staff in an aging healthcare market.

Dental assistants, hygienists, GP assistants, physiotherapists, care workers — vacancies stay open for months. Salary transparency is no longer optional, atmosphere and team culture become decisive. We build the employer story your patient site is missing.

The sector pain

Why healthcare hiring is extra hard

  • 188 dental practices in The Hague compete for the same assistants and hygienists
  • Aging workforce doubles up: growing demand meets simultaneous exit of experienced staff
  • Patient sites convince patients, not colleagues — no dedicated employer story
  • Salary transparency is expected; without indication you get structurally fewer quality responses

How we solve it

The SIRCLE Model applied to healthcare

  • Strategy: Employer brand DNA — what sets your practice apart as employer?
  • Production: Dedicated career site, team interview video, role-specific landings
  • Growth: Channel choice based on audience — Google for active searchers, social where it fits
  • Care: Continuous per-platform reporting, optimize cost-per-application
