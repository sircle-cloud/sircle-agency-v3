Challenges in hospitality & events

WHAT HOSPITALITY DEMANDS VISUALLY

Four pain points we encounter with every hospitality or event client.

Atmosphere is hard to capture

What works in person flattens online. Finding the right visual language for your venue or event is a craft of its own.

Content production never ends

Opening weekends, seasonal menus, events, the demand for content is high and continuous. One production day per year isn't enough.

The website sells the first experience

Visitors decide on imagery. An outdated site or weak photos cost you bookings before they begin.

Aggregators take direct bookings

TheFork, Booking, Ticketmaster, they take the margin. Your own platform with direct booking makes the difference.

Employer Branding for hospitality

700+ vacancies in Scheveningen. You get no responses.

The hospitality staff shortage is structural and peaks in summer. Beach clubs, restaurants and hotels compete for the same Gen-Z workforce. Whoever has the best employer story wins. We build that story.

The sector pain

Why hospitality hiring is extra hard

  • 30,000+ open NL hospitality vacancies, structural shortage through 2040 (UWV)
  • 60% of staff is 15-25 years old — searches first on TikTok and Instagram, not job boards
  • Hospitality school enrollment dropped 6,000 students/year over 10 years
  • Summer peak concentrates demand in 12 weeks — no time to slowly build

How we solve it

The SIRCLE Model applied to hospitality

  • Strategy: What sets your venue apart as employer — vibe, team, development?
  • Production: Career site, employer brand video with team in action, location photos
  • Growth: Channel choice tailored to audience per role — from management to kitchen staff
  • Care: Continuous campaign optimization, quickly add new vacancies
