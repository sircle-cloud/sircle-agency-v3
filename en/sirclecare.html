Why maintenance?

WHAT IF YOU DO NOTHING?

A website without maintenance is like a car without a service. It will break down. The only question is when.

Website hack

Outdated plugins are the #1 cause of hacks. If your site isn't maintained, it's not a question of "if" but "when".

Slow website

Without optimisation, your site keeps getting slower. Slow sites lose visitors, conversions and Google rankings.

No backups

Without backups, a hack or crash can wipe everything: content, structure, SEO rankings. Everything.

SEO decline

Google penalises insecure and slow sites. Without maintenance you steadily slide down in search results. Invisible to your customers.

SSL & legitimacy

An expired SSL certificate = "insecure connection" in the browser. Visitors bounce instantly. Trust gone.

"It's all on you"

Without a maintenance partner, you're personally responsible for updates, security, hosting and troubleshooting. Costs you unnecessary time and stress.
