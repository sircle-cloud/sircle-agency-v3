FIT IN WASSENAAR

A healthy and happy life in Wassenaar — for everyone. Brand, mini-docu, press photography and the central platform of the municipality's prevention programme. Multi-year creative partner.

Fit in Wassenaar is the prevention programme of the Wassenaar municipality, connecting residents, sports clubs, schools and care providers around one goal: a healthy and happy life for everyone. We've been the ongoing creative partner since 2023 — from brand and strategy to platform, video, press photography and execution.

At the heart of the work is a central online platform where residents find their way to sport, exercise, support and mental health. A large database that opens up the full local offering — for residents, and for organisations that want to join in.

Fit in Wassenaar — Mini Docu

fitwassenaar.nl — the central platform. Residents wanted clarity, the municipality wanted to connect — we built the bridge. An interactive database where residents discover what's available for them: from sport and exercise to guidance and care. Everything on an interactive map, filtered by programme, target group and offering.

Built as a custom solution in a user-friendly CMS. Organisations manage their own profile, content grows organically, and everything is searchable for residents looking for the right exercise, help or sports club nearby.

Lentedag 2025 — aftermovie

PR activation: 1,592,298 steps. On 19 and 20 March 2026 Wassenaar kicked off its role as a JOGG municipality with a two-day outdoor sports relay. Concept, campaign design, press approach, photography and film production: Sircle. The brief: build an action that beats last year's record (704,709 steps, St. Bonifaciusschool) and reaches the regional press. Outcome: over 1.5 million steps — nearly doubled — with coverage by NPO Zapp Jeugdjournaal, Omroep West, AD Regio Wassenaar, Centraal+ and De Wassenaarder.

Educational animations — Kansrijke Start. Short 2D animations that walk parents through young child development. On-brand, in a friendly visual language, designed to share standalone via social and embedded on the site alongside the related flowcharts.
