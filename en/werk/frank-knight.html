Logistics property that sells through imagery. For Knight Frank we produced the complete visual set for Tasmanweg Venlo, a new-build distribution centre with Rhenus Logistics as main tenant.

Knight Frank works with international investors in industrial real estate. For the Tasmanweg engagement in Venlo, a new distribution centre with Rhenus Logistics as main tenant, we delivered the complete visual set for brochures, website and pitch presentations.
