Client: Vivium Zorggroep Construction documentation · Drone · Video

VIVIUM · NAARDERHEEM

A new build you'll want to look back on. For Vivium we document the construction of Naarderheem, from the first pile to delivery, in cinematic drone and ground shots.

Vivium Zorggroep is building a new generation of healthcare real estate at the Naarderheem location, an engagement spanning years. For a project like this, construction documentation isn't a luxury but an investment: proof of progress for stakeholders, press and residents, and a lasting visual archive of what was built.

We film periodically on location with our own drone and ground crew. Each edition gets its own 45-second cinematic edit that captures the state of play: from foundation phase to the first floors. Not a contractor's construction clip, but material that matches the look of a serious healthcare organisation.
