Client: Outdoor Therapie Branding · Web · Photo · Video

OUTDOOR THERAPIE

Growing outwards. Since 2021 we've been building brand identity, website and visual content for a unique combination of outdoor adventure and therapeutic guidance.

Outdoor Therapie combines what rarely meets: outdoor adventure and therapy. Since its founding in 2021 we've been building the brand, from brand identity and logo to website, photoshoot and on-location cinematic content in the mountains.

The branding breathes calm and earthy tones, a palette drawn from the mountain landscape, a clear typeface, a logo that unites nature and human growth. The website takes visitors on a journey: serene photography, a clear colour scheme, easy paths to contact and bookings.

Brand Strategy Logo & Identity Website Cinematic Video Drone Footage Photography Ongoing Partner

On location in the mountains, original photography and drone footage during a multi-day expedition. The visual DNA of Outdoor Therapie stands or falls with these images.
