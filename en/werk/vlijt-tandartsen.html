VLIJT TANDARTSEN

Smiling is better living. From a new name to the fastest-growing dental practice in Eindhoven.

Three dentists came in for a name. It became Vlijt, a complete brand strategy and full digital foundation: visual identity, website, original photography and video, print and growth via Google, Meta and local SEO.

Behind the scenes

Custom CRM for data-driven growth

Off-the-shelf software didn't fit, too complex, too expensive, not designed for a dental practice's workflow. So we built a bespoke CRM portal in three weeks. Excel sheets and Google Spreadsheets out, real-time insight in.

  • Live sales pipeline, from first inquiry to enrolled patient
  • Postcode heatmap with patient locations across Eindhoven and surroundings
  • Source tracking per marketing channel (Google Ads, SEO, social, word-of-mouth)
  • GDPR-compliant from day one, with clear authorisations per team member
