Sircle Solutions

Custom booking platform on Airtable + Stripe

Off-the-shelf WooCommerce didn't fit — supervision sessions, reading time and group courses each have their own flow. So we built a dedicated checkout platform on boeken.breinwijzers.nl with Airtable as the operational database and Stripe for payments.

  • Custom checkout with Individual, Duo, Group and Events streams
  • Real-time availability from Airtable per supervisor
  • Automatic intake and calendar coupling per supervisor (Maartje, Mareille, Femke, Marte, Marjolein, Renske)
  • Payments via Stripe, no separate accounting tools needed
