KANSLOKAAL

From Schooladviesdienst Wassenaar to Kanslokaal. Complete rebrand with naming, DNA sessions and website.

Schooladviesdienst Wassenaar has existed since 1979, active for schools, the municipality and residents. We rebranded the organisation completely: new name Kanslokaal, tagline "A step forward for everyone", visual identity, website and print.
