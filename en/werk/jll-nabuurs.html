Client: JLL Investment Memo · Brand application · Print

JLL · DC KOKERBIJL

An investment memo that opens a deal. For JLL we designed the Prime Logistics Investment Opportunity brochure for DC Kokerbijl (Nabuurs HQ, Haps) — brand-compliant, print-ready and deadline-sensitive.

JLL is one of the world's largest real estate advisors. Their investment memos go to international investors with tight attention spans — the first 30 seconds decide whether a deal moves forward. So this brochure had to be brand-compliant, scannable and visually strong all at once.

We designed the full document for DC Kokerbijl — a prime logistics opportunity with Nabuurs as main tenant. 21 spreads: cover, key facts, location, macro and micro cartography, technical specs, tenancy details, financials and contact. In-house drone photography + custom cartography + info design — all in JLL house style. Print-ready and as PDF for digital distribution.

Investment Memo Brochure Design Cartografie Drone Fotografie Info-design Brand Application Print & PDF Logistics Vastgoed

Key Facts spread. Investment data readable in 30 seconds. Two-column table with fund specs and tenant info, layered on the drone aerial with the signature yellow JLL accent.

Cartography as a sales tool. Two custom-drawn maps of the Dutch–German logistics corridor: a macro overview (Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Düsseldorf with airports and motorways) and a micro zoom on DC Kokerbijl with drive times to surrounding cities, motorways, inland terminal and airports. All bespoke-illustrated — no Google Maps screenshots.

Site context + technical description. DC Kokerbijl in its neighbourhood context: labelled drone shot with adjacent blue-chip tenants (Danone, Foot Locker, DSV Solutions). Then a full-bleed aerial with info design on sustainability — BREEAM Very Good, climate-neutral HQ, 1,440 solar panels.

Overview spread. Detail specs (warehouse, mezzanine, office, parking) layered on a drone shot. Two units, identical layout — readable without horror vacui.

Custom cartografie Macro + micro-location maps op maat
Drone-fotografie Eigen aerial-shoot van het complete distributiecentrum
