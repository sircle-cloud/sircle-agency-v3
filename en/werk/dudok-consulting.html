DUDOK CONSULTING

Personal brand for business coach & leadership specialist Gwen Dudok van Heel. Branding, original photoshoot, website and, what makes the difference, an ongoing LinkedIn content programme with provable results.

Gwen Dudok van Heel is a business coach and leadership specialist for entrepreneurs and executives. Strong story, strong method, but her online appearance no longer did the work justice. We built a new personal brand: rebrand, original on-location photoshoot, a fast website (Lighthouse 100/100), and since then an ongoing partnership with email marketing and weekly LinkedIn content.

Three years on, the brand keeps growing. Weekly posts, sharper positioning, more leads from organic and social. One partner, one visual line, one result the numbers show.
