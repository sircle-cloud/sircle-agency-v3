Client: De Zelfspot Branding · Web · Foto · Video

DE ZELFSPOT

Scheveningen's living room. Branding, website with custom ticketshop, photoshoot and aftermovie for a community space for personal development and wellbeing.

De Zelfspot is Scheveningen's living room, a space for personal development, wellbeing and community. For them we built a user-friendly website with a custom ticketshop where visitors can easily sign up for various activities and sessions.

Alongside we shot the photography on location (together with Roast Factory) and a short atmospheric video to carry the story. One consistent visual story for website, social and print.

Branding Website Ticketshop Photografie Video Content

On location, atmospheric stills from an evening at De Zelfspot and a photoshoot together with Roast Factory. Imagery that carries the community side of the brand.
