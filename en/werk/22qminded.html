22QMINDED

Brand strategy, positioning, brand film and webshop for a brand that strengthens the 22q community.

For Care4Minds we work on 22qMinded — a brand giving the 22q community a serious voice. We delivered the brand strategy and positioning, a brand film that carries the story, and a WooCommerce shop for merchandise. Since launch we provide ongoing support via SircleCare.

22qMINDED — brand film

Editorial photoshoot. On-location portraits of Linda and Sasja in their working environment — for brand, website, social and the brand film. One day, one visual line, years of usable material.

Brand film production. Studio recordings in The Hague — interview setup, in-house camera crew, time for the story. The brand film is the extension of the photoshoot: one look, one feeling, one message.

Website + WooCommerce shop. Own domain at 22qminded.com, on-brand, with a Dutch and Australian entry point for international guidance. Plus a WooCommerce shop for webinars, training and books — direct via a payment flow with bundle pricing.
