Client: World Skate Center Den Bosch · Tramkade Concept · Production · Brand Film

WORLD SKATE CENTER

A high-impact brand film where sport, culture and creativity collide. We tell the story of the largest indoor skate hall in the Netherlands. Not through explanation, but through energy, people and moments.

The largest indoor skate hall in the Netherlands deserved a film as layered as the venue itself. Five months of concept, shoots and edit. One film that feels like WSC: raw, layered, full of energy.

Concept Cinematography Interviews Editing Color Grading Sound Design
Where sport, culture and creativity collide.

Four chapters, one story

INTRO

What is World Skate Center?

A fast summary of the entire film. In one breath the viewer feels what this is about.

CHAPTER 01

Josephine's Journey

The personal story of an athlete who grows here. The human layer the whole brand rests on.

CHAPTER 02

High performance & events

From Josephine's story to the bigger stage: sports events, international competitions and high-level performance.

CHAPTER 03 · FINALE

Culture, events & community

The hall as a meeting place. Music, events, volunteers, friendships. The layer that makes WSC unique and brings the film home.

Bonus · credits reel

A short closing reel that brings all stakeholders who make WSC possible into view. Sponsors, partners, builders, coaches, volunteers. A tribute to the people behind the hall.
