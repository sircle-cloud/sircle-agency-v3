Our story

HOW IT STARTED

Sircle was founded in 2021 by Sebastiaan Vreeken, from one observation: brands deserve more than pretty pictures. They deserve a story, a plan, and someone who understands how to combine the two.

What began as a creative studio grew into a full-service agency with its own approach. Strategy gives direction. Creativity gives soul. We bring the two together.

Today we work with strategists, designers, developers and filmmakers. From brand strategy to website, from video to growth marketing. One team that gets your brand, not ten separate vendors.

Our impact

1% FOR THE PLANET

Since day one we donate 1% of our profit to Protect Our Winters, a global coalition of outdoor athletes, businesses and outdoor lovers driving systemic climate action. Not symbolic tree-planting, but lobbying, policy work and public debate in 17+ countries to actually bring emissions down.

Our contribution goes to POW Benelux, which amplifies the Dutch outdoor community's voice in The Hague and Brussels. Working with Sircle automatically opts you in. Small agency, small percentage, but every project adds up.
