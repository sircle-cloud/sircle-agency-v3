Channel choice

The right channel, for the right role

No fixed mix. We select per client and per role the channels that fit your audience. Sometimes that's just Google. Sometimes a combination of three or four platforms. Data leads, not dogma.

Google Ads

For active job-seekers

  • Catches people already searching
  • Strongest for experienced professionals
  • Role-specific landings

Meta Ads

For demographic + interest

  • Sweet spot 20-50 years
  • Video, photo, carousel formats
  • Works for all role types

TikTok Ads

For Gen-Z reach

  • Crucial for graduating students
  • Native video, short hooks
  • Low CPM for young audience

In the intro call we determine which channels make the most sense for your audience. No assumptions, no fixed templates.
