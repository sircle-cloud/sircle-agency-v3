Why this matters

SAME GROUND. NEW ERA.

Smith isn't leaving Amsterdam Zuidoost, it's stepping into a bigger, better version of it. The new headquarters sits minutes from where the company has worked since 1999. That's the real story here: not a relocation, an upgrade on home ground.

It comes at Smith's best year yet: market leader in chip distribution, riding the AI wave, roughly 50 people in Amsterdam and 1,000 worldwide. The current office at Alpha Tower has done its job, and once the movers leave for good, that chapter is gone. A generic relocation video won't carry any of that. A film built around it will.
